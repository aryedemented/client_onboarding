Ingredients:
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 3/4 cup buttermilk
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1 large egg
- 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions:
1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
2. In another bowl, mix buttermilk, milk, egg, and melted butter.
3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
4. Cook on a greased pan over medium heat for 30 seconds until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side.
5. Serve warm with syrup.
